Welcome to Learnnove

Learnnove helps students move from confusion to clarity using one connected learning flow. You practice with quizzes, complete assignments, and get recommendations based on real performance.

How It Works

  1. Enroll in your courses and start your learning plan.
  2. Attempt quiz and assignment tasks generated from enrolled subjects.
  3. Track marks, points, and progress inside your dashboard analyzer.
  4. Get focused recommendations to improve weaker areas quickly.

Why Students Prefer Learnnove

  • Performance-driven recommendations, not generic suggestions.
  • One dashboard for scores, progress, and personalized guidance.
  • Interactive quiz and assignment experience for continuous improvement.
